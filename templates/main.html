Welcome to the Mental Health Chatbot

Your Companion for Emotional Support and Well-Being

In today's fast-paced world, taking care of your mental health is more important than ever. Our Mental Health Chatbot is designed specifically for students, providing a safe space to talk about your feelings, seek guidance, and explore strategies for emotional well-being.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Interaction: Engage in meaningful conversations with our AI, which adapts to your mental health needs based on your responses and quiz results.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate easily through our intuitive design, allowing for a seamless chat experience.
  • Profile Customization: Create your unique profile, upload a profile picture, and track your mental health journey.
  • Confidential and Secure: Your privacy is our priority. All conversations are kept confidential, ensuring a safe environment to express yourself.
  • Accessible Resources: Access helpful resources and coping strategies tailored to your needs.

Whether you need someone to talk to, resources for mental wellness, or just a moment of reflection, our Mental Health Chatbot is here for you. Start your journey toward better mental health today!
